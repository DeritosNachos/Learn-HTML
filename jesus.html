Jesus Christ

The man who saved billions of lives

Here's a time line of Jesus' life:

  • 6 BC - Born in Bethlehem, Israel
  • 8 AD - Jesus Goes to the Temple to preach
  • 25 AD - Jesus is Baptized
  • 25 AD - Jesus is tempted by Satan in the Wilderness
  • 25 AD - Jesus performs his first Mircale.
  • 25 AD - Jesus Chooses his Disciples
  • 27 AD - Jesus Feeds 5000 People
  • 27 AD - Jesus Walks on Water
  • 27 AD - Jesus raises Lazarus from the dead
  • 27 AD - The Sermon on the Mount
  • 27 AD - Jesus talks to Zacchaeus
  • 27 AD - Jesus enters Jerusalem
  • 27 AD - Jesus clears the temple
  • 28 AD - Jesus eats the Last Supper
  • 28 AD - Jesus is cruicifed
  • 28 AD - Jesus ascends to Heaven.
